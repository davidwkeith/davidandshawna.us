David grew up in Vermont and Massachusetts, went to college in Iowa, and lived in Minnesota for seven years before moving to California in 2006.  He has been developing web sites since high school, and after a few years in retail (first at Ultimate Electronics, and then at Apple as an Assistant Manager and Genius), he came to Apple headquarters and got back to coding.  The first two years he developed tools for Apple retail, and since 2008 he has been writing code for developer.apple.com.


Shawna hails from Overland Park, Kansas, a suburb of Kansas City.  She got her journalism degree and promptly went into web content development for the University of Kansas, About.com, and then MacResQ (an Apple reseller).  When Apple opened a retail store in Kansas City, Shawna became a Mac Genius, and after a few years found an ideal role with Apple in Cupertino.  Since 2008 she has been a technical writer for AppleCare, creating service manuals and training for Apple technicians.
